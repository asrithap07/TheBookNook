Mystery

Prepare for a ton of revelations!

A Good Girl's Guide To Murder

Holly Jackson

The case is closed. Five years ago, schoolgirl Andie Bell was murdered by Sal Singh. The police know he did it. Everyone in town knows he did it. But having grown up in the same small town that was consumed by the murder, Pippa Fitz-Amobi isn't so sure. When she chooses the case as the topic for her final year project, she starts to uncover secrets that someone in town desperately wants to stay hidden. And if the real killer is still out there, how far will they go to keep Pip from the truth?

A Study In Charlotte

Brittany Cavallaro

Jamie Watson reluctantly receives a rugby scholarship to Sherringford, a Connecticut prep school near his estranged father. Complicating matters, Sherringford is also home to Charlotte Holmes, Sherlock's brilliant yet volatile descendant. Despite Jamie's inclination to admire her from afar, they become rivals upon meeting. A Sherringford student's suspicious death, reminiscent of Sherlock Holmes stories, forces Jamie to abandon caution. Framed for murder, Jamie and Charlotte must rely on each other. As danger mounts and trust becomes scarce, their lives intertwine in a perilous quest to clear their names.

Ace of Spades

Faridah Àbíké-Íyímídé

Welcome to Niveus Private Academy, a world of wealth and perfection shattered by a mysterious texter named Aces. In this riveting thriller, Devon, a talented musician, is exposed when private photos become public, while head girl Chiamaka's pursuit of power comes at a heavy cost. As secrets unravel, they discover someone is targeting them both, holding all the cards. This is no ordinary high-school game; it's a perilous battle against an unseen enemy. An exceptional YA voice delves into institutionalized racism, delivering an incendiary and compelling narrative with a shocking twist.

Firekeeper's daughter

Angeline Boulley

Daunis Fontaine, a biracial outsider from her hometown and nearby Ojibwe reservation, faces adversity due to her scandalous background. When tragedy befalls her family, she sacrifices her dreams to care for her ailing mother. Amidst the challenges, she finds solace in Jamie, a charming newcomer on her brother's hockey team. However, Daunis becomes a witness to a shocking murder, thrusting her into a dangerous criminal investigation. She reluctantly agrees to go undercover, but the web of lies and deaths intensifies, endangering her loved ones. In a heart-wrenching dilemma, Daunis must decide how far she will go to protect her community, even if it means shattering the only world she has ever known.

If We Were Villains

M. L. Rio

After a ten-year prison sentence, Oliver Marks is released, uncertain if he committed the murder that put him behind bars. Detective Colborne, the man responsible for his imprisonment, approaches Oliver on his release day, seeking the truth about what really happened years ago. Oliver was part of a group of seven actors studying Shakespeare at an esteemed arts college. Onstage and off, they assumed roles like heroes, villains, and temptresses. However, when casting changes and secondary characters rise to prominence, the plays blur into reality, resulting in a death. Now, faced with their greatest acting challenge, they must convince the police and themselves of their innocence.

Little White Lies

Jennifer Lynn Barnes

When Sawyer Taft's estranged grandmother appears at her door with a lucrative offer to participate in debutante season, she hesitates but ultimately agrees. Immersing herself in high society might uncover the mystery of her father's identity. Amid makeovers, lavish dresses, and hidden agendas, Sawyer finds unexpected friendship among scandalous debutantes with secrets of their own. As she delves deeper, Sawyer realizes her family isn't the only one with skeletons in the closet. Navigating treacherous relationships and unearthing the truth about her origins, Sawyer's journey in Little White Lies blends a fish-out-of-water tale with a layered mystery set in a world of grand estates and enigmatic debutante balls.

The Inheritance Games

Jennifer Lynn Barnes

To claim her inheritance, Avery must reside in Hawthorne House, a labyrinthine mansion filled with secret passages and the late billionaire's puzzles. Complicating matters, the mansion is also occupied by the family Tobias disinherited, including the four grandsons who expected to inherit billions. Grayson, the heir apparent, sees Avery as a fraud and vows to expose her. Meanwhile, Jameson views her as their grandfather's final enigma. Trapped in a world of privilege and peril, Avery must play the game to survive in this gripping tale of wealth, danger, and puzzles.

These Violent Delights

Chloe Gong

In 1926 Shanghai, the city is engulfed in chaos and debauchery, torn apart by a blood feud between two rival gangs. Juliette Cai, the proud heir of the Scarlet Gang, and Roma Montagov, her first love and betrayer, are at the center of the conflict. But when both gangs succumb to instability and a mysterious contagion that drives them to madness, a greater threat emerges. As deaths multiply, Juliette and Roma must put aside their guns and grudges to save their city from destruction. This heart-stopping debut combines a 1920s Shanghai setting, rival gangs, and a lurking monster in the Huangpu River.

They Wish They Were Us

Jessica Goodman

In the seemingly perfect world of Gold Coast, Long Island, high school senior Jill Newman discovers that appearances can be deceiving. Three years ago, her brilliant friend Shaila was killed by her boyfriend, Graham. With the case closed, Jill tries to move on. Now a senior and a member of an elite secret society called the Players, Jill is determined to make this year her best. However, when she receives texts questioning Graham's guilt, her perfect senior year crumbles. Determined to uncover the truth, Jill risks her friendships and future. Will she unravel the mystery of Shaila's death and the real culprit?

Truly Devious

Maureen Johnson

Ellingham Academy, a renowned private school in Vermont, was established by tycoon Albert Ellingham as a place where learning is a game. But shortly after its opening, his wife and daughter were kidnapped, leaving behind a chilling riddle signed by "Truly, Devious." Years later, true-crime enthusiast Stevie Bell arrives at the academy with a plan to solve the cold case. However, as she navigates her new school life and interacts with her diverse housemates, Truly Devious reemerges, and death once again strikes Ellingham Academy. In this first installment of the Truly Devious series, interwoven mysteries unfold, driving Stevie's relentless pursuit of justice across multiple books.
